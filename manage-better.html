Strategy Planning

As small and mid-sized companies grow, their leaders often struggle to define the right strategy to expand into new markets, scale up operations and professionalise the management team. Get the right team to help you address the hurdles. Get the GoAnalytics team.

Have you implemented any of the following five essential strategy steps?
  1. Have you documented a 2 to 3-year and a longer 4 to 5-year vision for your company?
  2. Do you know why and how to conduct regular Strength Weaknesses Opportunities and Threats (SWOT) analysis of your business?
  3. Do you know how to create and leverage an advisory board of members with varied and external business backgrounds?
  4. Do you have a documented business continuity plan?
  5. Have you documented your key risks and come up with a written contingency plan to make sure your business can deal with unexpected interruptions?

These five key strategy steps are just a few we help with that separate industry leaders from their peers. GoAnalytics can help you evaluate and fill any gaps.

Financial Planning & Control

A lack of financial management skills is a significant challenge that can limit growth and cause significant difficulty for growing your business. GoAnalytics engages with our expert team to help you untangle cash flow, deliver financial reporting and sort out money-management issues...

Here are some quick must haves to look at:
  1. What are your 3-6 key performance measures and how regularly do you and your decision-makers review them?
  2. Have you and your management team understood and mastered your cash flow?
  3. Have you set capital buffers and liquidity targets?
  4. Would you say you have an effective and robust, detailed financial reporting process in place?
  5. How close to maximum capacity will you have to operate to break-even?
  6. What level of sales will you need and when can you expect to be profitable?
  7. What changes can you make that could have the greatest impact on overall profits?
