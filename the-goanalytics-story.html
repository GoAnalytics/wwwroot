Our Story

A Client-Comes-First Approach

The two founding principals of GoAnalytics Consulting, Norman Son Kee and Marc Wright are certified management consultants and members of The Canadian Association of Management Consultants (CMC-Canada). Both have well over twenty years in consulting to industries such as Manufacturing, Retail, Services and Consumer Packaged Goods.

Norman brings his significant background and experiences in CFO and Controllership roles, having successfully engaged with many multi-national and global clients helping them improve financial control, productivity and overall performance. He also has Public and Global Management Accounting experience and holds an Executive Masters of Business Administration in Finance and Strategy from The University of Ottawa, Canada.

Marc complements the practice with his expertise and focus on Business Strategy, Optimisation and Corporate Performance Management—leveraging close to three decades of advisory services to Fortune 500 and global companies. He has held executive roles in several Canadian organisations and was also a Senior Business Strategy and Enterprise Solutions Consultant with Accenture PLC (the multinational management consulting services firm).

In 2001, Norman and Marc had a meeting in Ottawa, Canada and shared a vision for a new breed of consulting and a new kind of consulting firm—A consulting practice genuinely focused on putting the business needs of the client first and providing measurable value in every engagement.

GoAnalytics Consulting represents the genesis of this philosophy and shared vision. Norman and Marc agreed that putting clients first should mean each client succeeds and realises value. A successful portfolio of clients would ultimately lead to a successful consulting practice.
