About GoAnalytics Consulting

We are a boutique management consulting firm helping small and mid-sized companies to manage better and keep even more of their profits inside the business. We provide innovative solutions across industries such as Manufacturing, Food and Accommodation Services, Wholesale/Retail, Business Services, and Construction.

We boast a very nimble team of consultants that are domain experts, and industry thought-leads delivering value in every client engagement, passionate about creating exceptional client experiences, and delivering with excellence and integrity.

Our consultants are members of The Canadian Association of Management Consultants (CMC-Canada). That means you get competent practitioners, recognised and held accountable by a worldwide body of industry professionals.

How GoAnalytics Can Help

We Focus On Business-owner, C-level & Executive Management Needs

Regardless of the type of business you operate, we help you get the expertise and skilled resources necessary for reaching the next level in your business growth and performance.

You get help with implementing holistic plans that address the entire company or just a specific department/functional area.

We tailor your engagement based on your time constraints, your budget and your needs.

Typical engagements range from 30-, 60- or 90-days depending on the scope you decide. By the end of that time, we provide you with a solid plan that includes:

  • An analysis of your current business situation
  • A clear definition of your objectives
  • A realistic action plan to reach your goals
  • A documented budget underscoring the investment you need to make
