Optimize Operations

Just breaking even or even losing money? You're bringing in sales but your profits are falling short of expectations. Do you know why? Optimising operations to improve efficiency is one of the most important challenges facing high-growth businesses like yours...

Here are some quick Operations areas to consider:
  1. Review your suppliers' performance regularly. Define a clear set of measures that you use to evaluate each one. Tell each of them about your scorecard.
  2. Formalise each relationship with a signed and managed contract.
  3. Have a method to continually drive and develop new products and services.
  4. Have a process to address how you diversify across different markets and grow market share.
  5. Do you know your value-added activities and key causes of waste?
  6. How do you benchmark your business against the competition?
